About VirtualAdventurers


Founded since 2007

VirtualAdventurers was founded in Argentina by Mr. Ong where he had a passion to allow people who are keen into going for extreme holiday adventures but do not have the chance to do so by creating a virtual vacation specifically for adventures and people to experience extreme adventures virtually instead of travelling to places for it. Most of the virtual holiday adventures found in the website are extreme adventures but are exicting at the same time!.

VirtualAdventurers target is to have people to have extreme adventures at home by offering an unusual extreme holiday experience with tons of exicting adventures through a virtual reality simulation subscription! Not only that we often give good offers for people that are interested to buy our subscription to use our virtual reality simulation keep an eye out on our promotion section!
